About Us

Swift Home Maintenance

Swift Home Maintenace are expert roofing contractors, property maintenance and home-improvement service providers. We provide a wide range of services to both residential and commercial customers throughout County Kerry, County Limerick and County Clare.
Our professional and reliable team have over 15 years experience delivering exceptional work with quality materials. We are fully insured and provide a guarantee on all of our work, giving you peace of mind.
We do it all-complete roofing services, chimney services, emergency storm damage repair, uPVC specialists & guttering, pergolas & decking. All our services are tailored to meet the needs of our customers, at affordable prices. We aim to complete the job to the highest standards, in the shortest timeframe, to ensure you and your home are disturbed for the least possible time.
